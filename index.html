Frequently asked

Answers, up front.

Is this free to use?

Yes. Unlimited downloads, no account, no paywall. We don't run display ads in the flow either.

Where are the files stored?

They're not. Everything is processed in-flight and streamed straight to your device. We don't keep a copy and we don't log URLs.

What's the maximum video quality?

We deliver whatever the source exposes — typically up to 4K 60fps on YouTube and 1080p on Instagram. Pinterest video pins download at their native resolution.

Can I download private or login-gated content?

No. If the post requires an account or is private, we can't access it. This is a deliberate limit, not a bug.

Is this legal?

Downloading your own content, public-domain material, or content covered by fair use is generally fine. Respect the creator and the platform's terms — you're responsible for how you use the file.

Do you support other platforms?

Right now it's YouTube, Instagram, and Pinterest. TikTok, Twitter/X, and SoundCloud are on the roadmap.
